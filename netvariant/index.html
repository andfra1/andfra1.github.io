Our products

NV MessengerTM

enterprise ready messaging platform

The NV Messenger™ platform is a next generation messaging gateway designed to fit any business while ensuring minimal efforts and costs. Its superb graphical user interface, feature set and pricing make it the ideal choice among available messaging gateways.

NV SelfcareTM

sms subscription customer care software

The NV SelfCare™ platform acts as a service hub that interfaces and communicates with all the operator’s core systems thus providing the end user with a magnificent and seamless experience.

online banking platform

NV Banker™ is a full fledged, back-end independent, online banking platform that would take a banks service offering to a whole new level. The platform comprised of 3 subsystems: INTERNET BANKING, MOBILE BANKING and SMS BANKING
